To see the forest
for the trees

: to not understand or appreciate a larger situation, problem, etc., because one is considering only a few parts of it

Finnish people are metsäkansa, the forest people. Forests are property, green gold. In Finnish folklore forests were places of vital resources but also mystery. We live in the forests, from the forests and go into the forest from the outside.
